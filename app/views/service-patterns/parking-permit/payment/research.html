User needs observed while testing and iterating

What we learnt

When choosing how to pay, some users were confused by the idea of a one-off direct debit. These users expected direct debits to either be multi-part payments or repeating payments.

Some also wanted to know if there were charges associated with making a credit card payment.

Most users were happy to provide their payment details, as they clearly understood the need for this. However, many objected to the need to re-enter personal data such as name and address that had been entered earlier in the transaction.

What we changed

Further information has been added to the payment options to address users' uncertainties about one-off direct debits and credit card fees.
