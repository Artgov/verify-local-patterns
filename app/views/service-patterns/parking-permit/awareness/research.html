User needs identified during the Discovery phase

As a resident applying for a permit, I need:

  • permission to park right away so that I don't get fined
  • to know which permit to apply for and how to apply for it
  • to know that it's safe to apply online so that I'm not too worried to do so

In most cases, users were aware of the need for permits. What was less clear was how or where to apply and exactly what permit to apply for. In areas with a two-tier local government structure, for example with a local and a county council, it wasn't always clear which to apply to.

When looking for information on applying for a parking permit most users visited the council website or phoned the council. Some visited GOV.UK, but did not always find information that was relevant for them, especially in multi-administration local structures.

It was at this point that users decided how to complete their application. A significant number of those we spoke to were less confident online, and lacked the confidence to complete a digital application process unaided.
