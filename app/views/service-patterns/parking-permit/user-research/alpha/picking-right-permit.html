Picking the right permit type

What we learnt

In some early testing of this page, some users assumed that because vistors permits were mentioned, that it would be possible for visitors to apply for a permit. The version tested at this point did not clarify who could apply for the visitor's permit.

What we changed

By supplementing the name of the pemrit with the details of who applies for it, we have resolved the confusion seen in earlier niterations
