Resident's permit start page

What we learnt

By the time users leave this page and commit to applying for their permit, they need to have a clear understanding of what they are commiting to and what they will get in return. This understanding allows uswers to start the online application process with confidence.

Earlier versions of this page presented this information in a single bullet list, however, this large block of relatively undifferentiated content was often not read by users.

There remains an unresolved issue about houses of multipe occupancy. Residents of these properties are usually eligible for free permits, however this technical terminology is poorly understood and confuses many users.

What we changed

The information that users need to start the application confidently has been broken down and is now delivered in smaller chunks.

Information about the parking zone is now provided on an earlier page, and the remaining information is provided in smaller chunks, with appropriate sub-headings.
