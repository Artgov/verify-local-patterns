Checking permit eligibility

What we learnt

Earlier iterations included pages to confirm eligibility to users. It was found that users assumed that as long as they were allowed to continue with the application, there wasn't a problem.

What we changed

In later iterations, users were allowed to continue, and only notified if there not eligible. This change reduced the overall length of the interaction for most users with no observable negative impact.
