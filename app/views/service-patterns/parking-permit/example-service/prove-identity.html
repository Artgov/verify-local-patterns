Verifying your identity

To apply for a parking permit, we'll need to verify your identity.

You'll receive your permit faster if you verify your identity with GOV.UK Verify.

To use GOV.UK Verify you'll need a UK passport, photocard driving licence or bank statement.

  • You must agree to share your details from GOV.UK Verify
Fail to verify journey

Other ways to apply

You can use this service without using GOV.UK Verify.

Your permit will take longer to arrive as additional checks will be required. You will also be required to upload a proof of address such as a council tax bill, tenancy agreement, utility bill or recent bank statement
