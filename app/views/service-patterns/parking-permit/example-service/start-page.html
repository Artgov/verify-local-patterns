Apply for a resident's parking permit in {{council.shortName}}

To park in {{council.parkingBoundary}}, you'll need a resident's parking permit.

Parking permits allow you to park anywhere within the boundaries of the city.

About this service

  • Each household is allowed up to {{permitMax}} permits
  • It costs £51 for a resident's permit for the first vehicle in your household. The second vehicle costs £81, and the third and fourth costs £153.
  • To apply, you'll need to prove you live in Argleton and provide details of your vehicle.
  • You won't receive a physical permit. Wardens will check your vehicle's registration number.
