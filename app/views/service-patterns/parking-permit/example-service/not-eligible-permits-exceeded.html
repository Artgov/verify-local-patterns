Residents of Argleton are eligible for one parking permit per address. Your address already has a parking permit, so you can not get another one.

If you don't have one it's possible that a former resident has a parking permit that needs to be cancelled.

Contact the council to get a former resident's permit cancelled or if you think there has been a mistake.
