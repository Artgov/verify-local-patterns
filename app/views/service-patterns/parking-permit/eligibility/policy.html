Users must apply through their local council for their bus pass, and prove that they are over the state pension age.

Councils can find out a user's address and their date of birth by using GOV.UK Verify, the government standard for identity assurance.

This section outlines best practice for designing a concessionary travel application around GOV.UK Verify.
