Users must apply to their local council for a resident's parking permit. They need to provide proof of their address and give vehicle details.

Councils can find out a user's address by using GOV.UK Verify, the government standard for identity assurance.

This section outlines best practice for designing a parking permit application around GOV.UK Verify.
