The government standard for identity verification is GOV.UK Verify. Using Verify, the user can securely prove their identity. The service can also receive the user's address and date of birth from GOV.UK Verify.

Councils should also offer alternative routes to access the service if a user chooses not to use GOV.UK Verify. This should include an online and offline alternatives.
