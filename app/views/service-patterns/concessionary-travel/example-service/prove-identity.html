Verifying your identity

To apply for an older person's bus pass, we'll need to verify your identity.

You'll receive your pass faster if you verify your identity with GOV.UK Verify.

To use GOV.UK Verify you'll need a UK passport, photocard driving licence or bank statement.

  • You must agree to share your details from GOV.UK Verify

Other ways to apply

You can use this service without using GOV.UK Verify.

Your pass will take longer to arrive as additional checking will be required.
