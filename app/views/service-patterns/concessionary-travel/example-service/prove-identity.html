To apply for an older person's bus pass, we need to verify your identity.

You'll receive your pass faster if you verify your identity with GOV.UK Verify.

  • You must agree to share your details from GOV.UK Verify

Other ways to apply

You can use this service without using GOV.UK Verify.

Your pass will take longer to arrive as additional checking is required. You will also be required to upload:

  • proof of address such as a council tax bill, a tenancy agreement, a utility bill or a recent bank statement
  • an identity document such as a birth certificate, a passport or a photocard driving license.
