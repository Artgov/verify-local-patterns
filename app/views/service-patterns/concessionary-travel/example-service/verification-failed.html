Apply for a bus pass without verifying online

You can still apply online for a older person's bus pass, but you'll need to scan and upload some documents.

To apply online you will need to provide:

  • Proof of address, such as a council tax bill or a recent utility bill or a tennancy agreement or a mortgage statement
  • Proof of age, such as a birth certificate, a passport or a driving license
