All councils in England must provide bus concession for older people.

The scheme has gradually been extended since its introduction and since April 2008 has provided free off-peak local bus travel to eligible older and disabled people anywhere in England.

Read the English National Concessionary Travel Scheme guidance from the Department of Transport. The service works differently in Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.
