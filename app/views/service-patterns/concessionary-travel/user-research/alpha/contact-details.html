Contact details

What we learnt

In early iterations, users were able to choose whether they would recieve notifications from the service. Altough none opted out of these notifications on purpose, some did so accidentally.

Early iterations also only allowed users to select a single contact type, yet some users wanted to select more than one.

Some expressed dissapointment at having to re-enter their phone enumber or email address.

What we changed

Changes were made to allow users to select multiple contact types and to ensure that contact details that have already been entered are displayed.

Where possible, these pages should be pre-populated with contact details entered earlier in the transaction to reduce the need for re-entering personal data.
